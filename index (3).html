About

Built for Founders Who Build for Bharat

🎯

What is Karo Pitch?

A curated pitch platform that connects early-stage founders directly with investors — through structured events and a live discovery platform.

💡

Why We Built This

Most startup funding flows to metro cities. Thousands of brilliant founders in smaller cities never get a fair shot. Karo Pitch changes that.

🌏

Our Mission

To become India's most accessible startup funding platform — one that truly serves founders from Tier-2, Tier-3 cities and beyond.

"Thousands of founders are building amazing businesses but simply lack access to investors. Karo Pitch connects these builders with the right investors through curated pitch events and an open discovery platform."

Process

How Karo Pitch Works

Four simple steps from application to funding. No gatekeeping, no jargon — just real founders meeting real investors.

01
📋

Apply With Your Pitch Deck

Fill in your startup details and submit your pitch deck. Takes less than 10 minutes.

02
🔍

Get Shortlisted by KaroStartup

Our team reviews every application personally. Shortlisted founders are notified within 7 days.

03
🎤

Pitch Live to Investors

Present your startup in a structured closed-room pitch event — just like a real investor meeting.

04
🚀

Raise Funding & Scale

Connect with interested investors, close your round, and take your startup to the next level.
